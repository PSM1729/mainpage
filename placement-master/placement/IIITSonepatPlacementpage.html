Placement Record

Record of 2019 Batch

Indian Institute of Information Technology Sonepat witnessed another good placement season for the Batch 2015-19 with approximately 82% students getting placed. 22 companies participated in the recruitment drive including top MNCs. Recognizing IIIT Sonepat competency in the IT sector, reputed MNCs like Adobe System, IBM, Q3 Technology, Posist Technology etc. offered coveted job profiles.

Average and Highest package

The average annual package of the batch is Rs. 8.19 Lakh as per the latest record. The highest package offered was INR 40.63 Lakh per annum.

Record of 2018 Batch

Our first batch of B.Tech students who graduated in 2018 was offered job opportunities by some of the best organizations in India. These included Fundwave, Oyo Rooms, Carwale, Gemini Solutions and Osmosys software solutions. This year, the number of companies that visited our campus increased manifold. We believe this a reflection of the quality of our students and of the education and orientation they have received at the Institute. Some of our multinational recruiters represented industries ranging from Consulting, E-Commerce, Hi-Tech., Hospitality / Healthcare, and included giants like Azcom Infosolutions Pvt. Ltd., WEP solutions Ltd., Aricent, flipkart and IBM.

Average and Highest package

The maximum annual package offered to our first batch B. Tech students by the companies is 23.84 lac while the average package for our first batch B.Tech students is 6.44 lac.
